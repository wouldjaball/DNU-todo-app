# Master Services Agreement

**Sales Monsters x Industrial Defender**

**Effective Date:** _______________
**Term:** 6 months from effective date
**Prepared by:** Aaron Englert, Sales Monsters
**Prepared for:** Industrial Defender (iDefender LLC)

## Overview

Sales Monsters ("SM") will provide SEO strategy, content creation, LinkedIn promotion, graphic design, and performance reporting services to Industrial Defender ("Client"). The goal is to increase organic search visibility, drive qualified traffic to industrialdefender.com, and support Industrial Defender's thought leadership position in the OT cybersecurity space.

## Services and Deliverables

### SEO Strategy and Content

**Monthly Retainer:** $3,033/month

| Deliverable | Quantity | Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| SEO articles (long-form, keyword-targeted) | 6 | Monthly |
| Blog posts (shorter-form, topical) | 3 | Monthly |
| SEO strategy and keyword research | Ongoing | Monthly |
| On-page optimization recommendations | As identified | Monthly |
| Google Search Console setup and monitoring | 1 setup + ongoing | Ongoing |
| Google Analytics tracking and monitoring | 1 setup + ongoing | Ongoing |
| Monthly performance report | 1 | Monthly |

SEO articles will be optimized for buyer-intent keywords relevant to Industrial Defender's core verticals, including power generation, electric utilities, oil and gas, manufacturing, water/wastewater, and building automation. Blog posts will cover timely industry topics, compliance updates, and thought leadership angles.

All content will be written to match Industrial Defender's brand voice and submitted for Client review and approval before publishing. Two (2) rounds of revisions are included per deliverable. Additional revision rounds will be billed at $65/hour.

### LinkedIn Promotion

**Campaign Setup Fee:** $450 per campaign
**Monthly Management Fee:** $750/month (covers up to 3 active campaigns)

An "active campaign" is defined as any LinkedIn advertising campaign that is live, in review, or paused-with-intent-to-resume during the billing period. Campaigns that are fully stopped and archived are not counted as active. A 4th concurrent active campaign requires a new campaign setup fee ($450) and increases the monthly management fee by $250/month for the duration of that campaign's activity.

| Deliverable | Detail |
|---|---|
| Campaign strategy and setup | Per campaign as needed |
| Audience targeting and segmentation | Included |
| Ad creative development | Included |
| Campaign monitoring and optimization | Included in monthly fee |
| Performance reporting | Included in monthly report |

LinkedIn campaigns will promote webinars, gated content, and thought leadership pieces to targeted audiences in Industrial Defender's key verticals. Campaign setup includes audience building, creative development, and tracking configuration.

### Graphic Design

**Hourly Rate:** $65/hour

Graphic design services are available as needed for:

- Blog and article featured images
- LinkedIn ad creative
- Social media graphics
- Infographics and data visualizations
- Presentation support materials

Design hours will be tracked and reported on each billing cycle. SM will provide estimates before beginning any design work exceeding 2 hours.

## Setup and Onboarding

The following will be completed during the first 30 days:

## Reporting and Communication

### Reporting

SM will deliver a monthly performance report covering:

- Organic traffic trends
- Keyword ranking movement
- Content performance (page views, time on page, engagement)
- LinkedIn campaign metrics (impressions, clicks, CTR, conversions)
- Progress against service-level targets (see Service-Level Commitments)
- Recommendations for the upcoming month

Reports will be delivered in PDF or Google Slides format by the 5th business day of each month for the prior month's performance.

## Service-Level Commitments

### Baseline Metrics

During onboarding (first 30 days), SM will document current organic traffic, keyword rankings, LinkedIn engagement, and lead volume. These baseline metrics will be recorded in Appendix A and signed off by both parties as the agreed starting point.

### Performance Targets

The following target ranges reflect expected outcomes assuming Client cooperation (timely approvals, site access, subject matter input) and no major external disruptions (algorithm changes, site outages, market shifts):

| Metric | 3-Month Target | 6-Month Target | 12-Month Target |
|---|---|---|---|
| Organic traffic growth | +10-15% | +15-25% | +40-60% |
| Target keywords in top 20 | X keywords* | Y keywords* | Z keywords* |
| LinkedIn campaign CTR | 0.35-0.50% | 0.40-0.65% | 0.50-0.75% |
| Content engagement (avg. time on page) | Baseline +10% | Baseline +20% | Baseline +30% |

*Specific keyword counts to be defined in Appendix A based on baseline keyword research.

### Remedies

If performance targets are missed for two (2) consecutive months for reasons within SM's control, Client will receive a 10% service credit applied to the following month's SEO retainer. Service credits are capped at one (1) per rolling six-month period. Factors outside SM's control include but are not limited to: search engine algorithm changes, Client website downtime, delays in Client approvals, changes to Client's product or market positioning, and third-party platform outages.

Stock imagery, premium SEO tools, and other third-party costs used in the delivery of services are absorbed by SM as part of the retainer and management fees unless otherwise agreed in writing.

## Term and Termination

This agreement covers an initial term of 6 months from the effective date. Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice. In the event of early termination, Client is responsible for payment of all work completed through the termination date plus any non-cancellable third-party commitments made on Client's behalf.

After the initial 6-month term, this agreement will continue on a month-to-month basis unless either party provides 30 days written notice of intent to discontinue.

Either party may terminate immediately for cause if the other party materially breaches this agreement and fails to cure the breach within 15 days of written notice.

## Content Ownership, Approval, and Transition

### Ownership

All content, keyword research, content calendars, audience segments, campaign configurations, and other work product created by SM for Client becomes the property of Client upon payment. SM retains the right to showcase work in its portfolio with Client approval.

All accounts created or managed on Client's behalf (including Google Analytics, Google Search Console, and LinkedIn Ads) are and remain Client's property. SM will maintain access only as needed to perform services under this agreement.

### Approval Process

All content will be submitted for Client review before publishing. Client will have 5 business days to review and provide feedback. If no feedback is received within 5 business days, SM will send a follow-up reminder. If no response is received within 2 business days of the reminder, content will be considered approved.

### Transition

Upon termination of this agreement, SM will cooperate with Client for a period of 30 days to transition all accounts, credentials, work product, and institutional knowledge to Client or Client's designated successor. Transition cooperation will be provided at SM's then-current hourly rate. SM will not delete, withhold, or restrict access to any Client-owned accounts or data.
